I'm a programmer currently working towards my BS in Computer Science. I always dreamt about what a career in software development would feel like but I have never developed software in a professional capacity, only personal projects where I was the sole developer. I have used linux for 15 years and have grown to love it strengths and understand its weaknesses. Lately, I have been focused on systems programming and hardware specifically in the areas of bare-metal, x86-64 linux, and also embedded linux.

The first programming language I learned was QBasic, on my IBM 286 clone running windows 95 back in 1995. I'm currently taking a deep dive into c programming and low level concepts.

When I have spare development time, I make game prototypes(code,art,music). and when I take a break from "the computer", I play retro console games on my RG353PS linux handheld, watch movies and read cyberpunk fiction.
